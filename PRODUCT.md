# Product — ההוק לשיווק

## Register
brand

## Users
Israeli business owners, marketers, and entrepreneurs who are spending money on ads or content without a strategy. They know they need to market better but don't know where to start. Price-sensitive but motivated — 290 ₪ entry point. They respond to specificity, authority, and proof.

## Product Purpose
Sales landing page for "ההוק לשיווק" — a paid online training course (290 ₪) about building a marketing strategy for video content. Same page flow as the sister product: quiz (qualification) → loading → VSL + full sales page with pricing.

## Brand Personality
Bold. Direct. Premium but approachable. Results-driven. Same TV Production voice: confident without arrogance, Israeli market directness.

## Anti-references
- Generic course/coaching landing pages (fake urgency, stock photos of laptops)
- Cheap course platforms (pixelated graphics, clip-art icons)
- Overly salesy copy that feels manipulative

## Design Principles
- Specificity converts: concrete claims beat vague promises
- Same brand, different product tier: connects visually to the free meeting page but stands on its own
- Earned trust before the ask: social proof and credentials before pricing
- Clear value stack: the 290 ₪ price should feel like a steal by the time they see it
